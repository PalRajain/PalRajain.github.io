Biography Pallavi Rajain is a research scholar in Management Department at D.C.R.U.S.T. . Her research interests include women entrepreneurship, sensory marketing and green marketing. She has worked as project fellow for N.R.D.W.P project Evaluation Study of NRDWP at D.C.R.U.S.T. She has worked as project fellow for MGNREGA project Preparing Perspective Plan for MGNREGA in Districts: Kaithal & Yamunanagar contracted to D.C.R.U.S.T.
